About Me

I am studying Biomedical Engineering in my 1A term at the University of Waterloo, and loving every minute of it. I am particularly interested in the design of prosthetics, which I was introduced to as part of my involvement with Biomechatronics Club. I would also love to learn more about medical device manufacturing and "bio-hacking". In my spare time, I like to practice Vinyasa yoga and write short stories.
